# VAT Rates Reference

## Legal Basis

**Article 9, Law 48/2024/QH15** (VAT Law, effective 01/07/2025)

---

## VAT Rate Structure

### 0% Rate (Article 9(1))

**Goods and services subject to 0% VAT:**

| Category | Examples | Notes |
|----------|----------|-------|
| Exported goods | Goods sold and delivered outside Vietnam | Must be physically exported |
| Exported services | Services consumed outside Vietnam | Intangible services delivered to foreign recipients |
| International transportation | Air, sea, railway, road transport | Services rendered in international traffic |
| Goods transferred to bonded warehouses | Goods sent to customs-bonded zones | For further processing or re-export |
| Goods for diplomatic use | Official goods for foreign diplomatic missions | Subject to reciprocity conditions |
| Aid and humanitarian goods | Goods for aid programs | As designated by competent authorities |
| Aircraft and aircraft equipment | For international aviation | As regulated |
| Ships and marine equipment | For international maritime operations | As regulated |

**Legal Reference:** Article 9(1), Law 48/2024/QH15

---

### 5% Rate (Article 9(2))

**Essential goods and services:**

| Category | Examples | Notes |
|----------|----------|-------|
| Clean water | Tap water, purified water | For production and daily use |
| Fertilizers | Chemical fertilizers, organic fertilizers | For agricultural production |
| Pesticides | Herbicides, insecticides, fungicides | For agricultural production |
| Agricultural tools | Manual tools, simple machinery | For farming activities |
| Teaching equipment | Educational materials, school supplies | For educational institutions |
| Scientific equipment | Laboratory equipment, research tools | For scientific research |
| Medical equipment | Diagnostic equipment, surgical instruments | For healthcare |
| Assistive devices | Wheelchairs, prosthetics, hearing aids | For persons with disabilities |
| Press and publications | Newspapers, magazines, official publications | Subject to press law requirements |
| Art and cultural performances | Theater, music, dance performances | Domestic cultural events |
| Children's toys | Educational toys, recreational toys | For children |
| Books | Educational, scientific, cultural books | Textbooks, reference materials |
| Food products | Unprocessed or minimally processed | Natural state agricultural products |
| Protective equipment | Safety gear for production | For occupational safety |

**Legal Reference:** Article 9(2), Law 48/2024/QH15

---

### 8% Rate (Article 9(3)) - TEMPORARY REDUCTION

**Temporary rate per Decree 174/2025/ND-CP:**

| Application | Effective Period | Notes |
|-------------|-----------------|-------|
| Currently 10% rated goods/services | Until 31/12/2026 | Reverts to 10% after reduction period |

**Important Notes:**
- This is a temporary reduction measure to support economic recovery
- The rate reverts to 10% from 01/01/2027 unless extended by new regulation
- Not all 10% rated items may qualify—verify specific product/service classification
- This reduction applies to **domestic transactions** primarily

**Legal References:**
- Article 9(3), Law 48/2024/QH15
- Article 1, Decree 174/2025/ND-CP

**Action Required:** Monitor for extensions or modifications to this reduction period.

---

### 10% Rate (Article 9(4)) - DEFAULT

**Default rate applies to:**

All goods and services **NOT** covered by 0%, 5%, or 8% rate categories.

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| Manufacturing | Industrial production goods |
| Construction | Building materials, construction services |
| Trading | Retail, wholesale distribution |
| Services | Professional services, consulting |
| Hospitality | Hotels, restaurants (unless exempt) |
| Transportation | Domestic transport (not international) |
| Telecommunications | Communication services |
| Information technology | Software, IT services |

**Legal Reference:** Article 9(4), Law 48/2024/QH15

---

## Rate Determination Flowchart

```
Is the good/service exported or international transport?
├── YES → 0% (Article 9(1))
└── NO ↓

Is the good/service an essential necessity (Article 9(2))?
├── YES → 5% (Article 9(2))
└── NO ↓

Is the transaction subject to temporary reduction ( Decree 174/2025/ND-CP)?
├── YES → 8% (Article 9(3))
└── NO ↓

Default rate applies → 10% (Article 9(4))
```

## Compliance Checklist

When verifying VAT rate application:

- [ ] Identify the specific good or service being transacted
- [ ] Match against the four rate categories (0%, 5%, 8%, 10%)
- [ ] Cite the specific article and sub-article from Law 48/2024/QH15
- [ ] For 8% rate, verify the transaction falls within the reduction period
- [ ] Document the rate determination with legal reference

---

## Important Notes

1. **Rate Classification:** Some goods/services may fall under different categories depending on specific characteristics. Verify classification carefully.

2. **Composite Services:** When a transaction includes multiple elements, allocation may be required based on each component's rate category.

3. **Rate Changes:** The 8% temporary reduction will require monitoring for expiration or extension.

4. **Industry-Specific Rules:** Certain industries may have additional VAT treatment provisions not covered in this general reference.
